Peter's Track & Field page

Introduction

Last week I failed to win a medal in the National Indoor Track&Field Championships for Masters (that is: elderly athletes). It was the second time in a row that I missed out on the medals.

Since I started track running, almost 10 years ago, I had always won a medal at the Indoors Championships. But last summer I missed out on the medals during the Outdoor Championships for the first time since 2012. Are my running days over now?

Anyhow, I feel that this might be a good moment to start describing my Track and Field hobby.
